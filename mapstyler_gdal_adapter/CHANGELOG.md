## 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- Sync and isolate-based async vector loading APIs.
- Layer selection by index or name.
- OGR spatial and attribute filters.
- Geometry conversion for Point, LineString, Polygon and multi-geometries.
- Native-unit and meter-based geometry simplification.
- Multi-scale loading with multiple simplification levels.
- Layer metadata inspection.
- Non-fatal warnings for unsupported or empty geometries.
